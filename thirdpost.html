Are Guns too Easy to get?

Getting a gun can be as easy as walking up to a store and signing papers. What are ways we can better montior the purchases and keep guns out of the wrong hands?

Posted by Joshua Hoffman on April 26, 2023

Yes, owning guns is a constitutional right but are there too many of them out there for the government to track eccectivly? According to Phillip J. Cook in his article he states "Since the instant check system was put in place in 1998, over 3 million customers have been denied because they were found to be disqualified, and currently about 1.2% of all federal background checks result in denial." Having a 1.2% denial rate seems crazy low when looking at how many mass shooting could havebeen stopped with a indepth check on who is buying the weapon.

States around the USA are starting to adopt a law called "Red Flag Laws". These laws allow the government, by a judge order, to take away guns from people who are showing signs of mental illness symptoms or agression towards others.

Gun control laws like the "Red Flag" can be tied to stopping mass shooting or gun violence all around the country. Stricterregulations on how to get guns can save lives and protect people so we can stop wondering...When am I next?
